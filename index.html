Ok, so... What do we get when minting?

You get a unique FRENFT, the pixel art looks pretty close to how you look now.

Why pixel art?

The base NFT is pixel art, because the goal is to allow for ultimate customization, modability, and modification. Using a "lowrez" pixel art sprite allows future developers to modify these NFTs to be trading cards, 3D models, text-based companions, VR animations, and much much more. Keeping the base art simple, allows for maxium building possibility, while still being cute to begin with :3

What do we get besides the art?

These NFTs aren't just a "pfp" series, they carry rich metadata that will help build the future metaverse adaptations around it. Things like names, stats, lore, attributes, and more! Think of minting these NFTs like a baby, which will eventually grow into complex & incredibly interesting creatures. They evolve based on community engagement.

What incentivizes the community to build in this metaverse?

This is smartly done using a "carrot & stick" approach. People who participate in this community (building games, making apps, sharing tutorials, onboarding new users) will earn a companion token which, among other utility, will serve as a governance token in a DAO. This DAO will grant funding to new project proposals (that everyone is free to pitch & vote on), guide marketing efforts, and host interactive events (imagine a colosseum full of frens watching you have pokemon-like card battles with someone from around the world).

In addition to community efforts, I'm personally lining up partnership with other projects & teams, as well as building my own experiences that will help make FRENFTs an attractive & fun web3 adventure for everyone to participate in. More attention == More incentives == More frens == More fun.
